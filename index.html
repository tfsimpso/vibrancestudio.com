Our Work

Showcased below are examples of our work for clients.

Logo Design, Website
Evolvemint is a travel inspired, social luxury company in the eco fashion space. The Evolvemint logo was designed to be easily imprinted into leather goods and to represent the balance of fashion and story.
Mobile App UI Design
ADIT allows people with disabilities be able to search, visit, and rate local businesses based upon their accessability. The app tailors the ratings to the specific disability of the user. We helped the ADIT team design the unique user interface of their mobile application.
Logo Design, Video Web Element
During the Triangle Startup Weekend for Education, our team created EduLaunch, a crowd funding platform designed specifically for educational projects. We built the fully functional website and funded 4 out of the 6 projects in just three days.




Hungr Mobile App UI Design
Hungr is like Tinder for recipes as you can swipe right or left to like or dislike recipes. Hungr also selects recipes for you based on your preloaded preferences or health conditions. We helped them visualize their mobile application in a form they can easily share.
Email Template
Campus Cruizer is like Lyft or Uber specifically designed for Universities whose motto is “for students, by students”. They are working towards encouraging students to party responsibly and reducing instances of drinking and driving. We provided an email template to increase user conversion.
Logo Design, Mobile App UI
Papilia won people's choice award and second place overall in HQ Raleigh's Triangle Start-Up Weekend for Women. Papilia is a travel inspired fashion app that helps you save time and space packing for a trip to any destination.

Our Process

Here is an overview of how we approach each new project.

Plan

  • Client interview
  • Gather consumer data
  • Create strategy
  • Analyze research

Create

  • Build test layout
  • Gather client feedback
  • Code/Design development
  • 3 iterations of critique

Launch

  • Deploy project
  • Send files
  • Answer questions
  • Close contract
